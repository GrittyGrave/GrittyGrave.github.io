How to use it: chopping a loop into 16 pieces? Use the 1/16 note value. Setting a delay to sync with the beat? Use 1/8 or dotted 1/8. Fading a sample tail? Match it to 1/4. These ms values are what your DAW or MPC wants.

Tip: assumes 4/4 time (the standard for hip-hop). "Bars" means how many measures the sample loops over — most drum breaks and sample loops are 2 or 4 bars. If the BPM comes out weird, try doubling or halving your bar count.
