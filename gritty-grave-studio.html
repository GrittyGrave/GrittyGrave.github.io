🥁 Sequencer

Step 2
📖 How to use the Sequencer
  • Click any step to toggle it on/off
  • Send to Sequencer from the MIDI Maker above to auto-load a pattern
  • Melody row — click a step to place a note, then pick the pitch below the grid
  • Adjust BPM with the ▲▼ arrows or type a value
  • Swing — adds groove to even 8th positions
  • Hit Play ▶ to hear your pattern loop
  • Clear row — the × button wipes a single row
  • Export .mid — download the full sequencer pattern as a MIDI file
